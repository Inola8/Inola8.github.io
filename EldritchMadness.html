About the game

Game

In this game you play as a lantern fish trying to survive in a twisted and harsh ocean. After chemicals and toxic waste have tempered with the ocean for good, the creatures in the deep dark have gone wrong. Mutated, glowing fish have turned on eachother, using everything in their power to live. Laserbeams, flying light bulbs, a twisted Kraken, you name it. You just got to be quick and survive.
This is a classic SHMUP game with a 3D style and an underwater theme. This project was made with 3 developers and 4 artists, we made this in 4 weeks, during this time I was the dev lead.

    Boss fight: pick attack

    I made the kraken boss as the final battle, it has three different attack states. First, it will only start fighting if the player is close enough, from there on it will randomly pick one of three states, with a short interval. The attacks are a big waterbeam, a whip of a tentacle, and a random ship item being thrown at the player.

    Boss attacks

    Here are two of the attacks. They are pretty simple, for the item throwing I made a List of objects to throw and point an empty spawner at the player, the instantiated objects have some forward force. The other two attacks are basically the same expect for the visuals, I just activate the visual and enable a boxcollider.

    Player take damage

    We really wanted to play with the fact that the player is a lantern fish, so we made some functions in the GameManager that will be triggered when the player takes damage. So when the player kills enemies, his light will shine brighter, but when he misses his light will decrease.
