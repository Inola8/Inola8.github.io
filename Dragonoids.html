About the game

Game

This is my take on Asteroids, another basic game I made in 2 weeks. I wanted to make it dragon themed, with a vulcanic vibe. I added my own twist by implementing abilities with a cooldown, and baby dragons who will help you for a little while. Instead of a boss I wanted to give the player a different challenge; they have to collect 3 shield pieces so they will not get crushed by the meteorshower.

    Ability cooldown

    To spice this simple project up I added abilities with cooldowns, so here is one of them. So I take a Sprite Image and set the fill method to Radial 360 so it fill clockwise. Upon pressing the assigned button I activate the right ability Image. The fillAmount will go from full to empty via a timer - which is different for all of the abilities, once it is 0, it can be activated again.

    High score

    At the start of the game the player may enter their name, which I store in PlayerPrefs and will be displayed on the endscreen. Here I also show the score and highscore.

    Spawning of shield pickups

    When the player reaches a score of 600 the 'boss fight' will be triggered, which is a meteorshower. The player will have a limited time to pick up three shield pieces to gain immunity to this shower, or it will be game over.
