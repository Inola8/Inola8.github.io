About the game

Game

In this game you are a very shy person, meaning you do not want anyone to see you! Try to make your way back home from school without getting noticed. I made this game by myself in 2 weeks. It is a point and click game, with some keyboard controls for camera movement and interacting with the door.

    NPC FOV

    To check if the NPCs can see the player, I used an OverlapSphere, but only within a certain angle, which I just kind of played around with until it felt right. The NPC will respond to seeing the player, which it basically game over for the player.

    Door!

    To check for the doors and keys I made an interactable layer. The player will check for anything on this layer to interact with if the right key is pressed. For the door it will change states. The key will simply be put in the inventory, or removed.

    Moving NPC

    This NPC has a certain radius in which it will look for a spot to walk to, wait for a little while, then move to the next spot within the same radius.
