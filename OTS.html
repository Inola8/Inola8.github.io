About the game

Game

During this project I worked together with a student from the film academy in Amsterdam as the artist. We made this story about a person with social anxiety trying to mingle with his peers, but he keeps failing to do so, so he escapes to his safe space - the game world. So he tries to gamify this evening trying to socialize with the other party members. This was my first project in Unreal Engine using Blueprints. It was a key element for this other student to use meta humans to give it a more realistic feel. We brought this experience together in 8 weeks.

    Interact with NPC

    The NPCs have icons above their head, I made it so they always face the player, here I disable the arrow above the head when the player starts interacting with an NPC, when the NPC finishes talking, the cross icon will be enabled.
    I trigger the anxiety after a delay to build it up, then I let it slow down again when the conversation is over.

    Dialogue text

    For the text inside of the dialogue boxes I have an array with a lot of lines, I randomly pick a line and set the text with a widget blueprint. Then to make sure the player can always read the text I added some light.

    Dialogue text lines

    Here are the arrays with options, I made it so each NPC picks lines from their own array, by comparing their Id with the index from Select. I also cast this the the widget blueprint.

    Anxiety effect

    For this I made my own vignette effect with an audio of a looping heartbeat, I made a slower and a faster version. I toggle between these to try and convey the feeling of anxiety.
