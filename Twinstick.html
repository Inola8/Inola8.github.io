About the game

Game

In this game you will play as a small robot, with one goal in mind: find your micro chip! You find yourself in an old museum, somewhere in this museum is the last technology humans made before extinction. only this can save your fellow robots. Make your way through the differently themed rooms, from the Dino time, to Egypt, a planetarium and see how your kind was made. Solve puzzles to open the door to the next room, and try not to get caught by the strange creatures that have come to life. Maybe if you're nice to your surroundings, you can find yourself a friend. Who doesn't love dino nuggies?
This was another group project with artists. Our team existed of two developers and two artists. During this project we rotated the SCRUM master role, so we all took responsibility for that. This game's core mechanic is supposed to be Twinstick, but we wanted to spice it up by making it a puzzle/ kinda stealth-y game rather than another shooter.

    Dino digging

    One of the things this Dino does is dig for a key. For this the player must first obtain a shovel. Then with some given hints the player has to figure out which key to press for the Dino to start digging.
    For the digging part I have the Dino move to a position where the animation of the shovel will play, and an object will spawn, plus the key will be added to the inventory.

    Lock & key 1

    When the player reaches the lock for the first door, it will check if the player has a key. Then some animations and lights will respond, and the key will be removed from the inventory.

    Pressure plate puzzle

    For this I search all of the tiles, then check if two tiles are being pressed. Two tiles have been checked as the correct ones in the inspector, if both of these are also pressed the riddle has been solved, else the guardian cat will start chasing you.

    Dino movement

    Simple Raycast used to set the destination of the Dino.
    The Dino has a spot behind the player that it will move to when the right button is pressed.
