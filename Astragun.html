About the game

Game

Immerse yourself in this magical world of your own miscreated creatures! Roaming around these dungeons you are the scientist who likes to experiment and push natures boundaries, but this time something has gone awfully wrong and your creatures have turned against you. Now it is time to grab your Astragun and defeat these monsters to get some justice. Take advantage of your environment and be wise with your assets, they are not unlimited!
/Group project made by 2 devs and 2 artists.

    Enemy spawning

    The enemies will spawn when the player enters a room, here the StartSpawning function gets triggered. I made a check so the WaveManager knows which spawner is the active spawner, so it will spawn enemies in the right room. Then I start spawning the enemies on a random spot. I link the enemies to the spawner, so they get added to the right List.

    Enemy base class

    I made a base class for all the enemies, so the specific enemies inherit these behaviors. The look at is just so the enemies will look at and move towards the player. At the spawn state I add the enemies to the List, in the die state they will be removed. In the move and attack states I simply check the distance between the enemy and player. Via a Scriptable Object per enemy I passed a stopping distance, which will decide when the enemy will move to the attack state.

    WaveManager

    In the WaveManager I just keep track of the current wave and max waves. the max waves will be different for each room, so I set that in the enemy spawner.

    Enemy boss attack

    So this boss has a couple of eyes attached to it, that fire lasers at the player. These eyes can be shot, they will detach and move to the player as a final attempt to do damage. If all of the eyes are detached, the boss will move on to an enraged state, then it will shoot a laser from it's main eye.
